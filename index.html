About me

I am a Computing science second-year Master's student at Simon Fraser University (SFU) and a member of GrUVi lab, where I am fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Ali Mahdavi-Amiri. Prior to this, I completed my undergraduate degree in Computer Engineering from Sharif University of Technology.

My research primarily revolves around computer vision and graphics, with a recent emphasis on neural fields and 3D generative modeling.
